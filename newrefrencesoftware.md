How QR Secure Works
Door-lock verification
🏷️
Step 1Create QR Site
Set up a client's door with a name, address, and a 4-8 digit PIN.
🖨️
Step 2Print & Laminate
Print the credit-card-sized QR label and place it near the normal exit point.
📱
Step 3Carer Scans QR
After locking the door, the carer scans the QR code on their phone.
🔐
Step 4Enter PIN & Confirm
They enter the PIN and confirm the door is locked and secure.
✉️
Step 5Notification Sent
An email and/or SMS is sent to the designated contacts confirming the door is secure.
